Modeling

As part of my job, I would sometimes have to stand in as a model. This could mean a lot of things, but mostly my duties were to stand and make sure the cleats were in the right position, clean, and looking good. As you can see below, I mostly shot with Adidas product by photographer Daniel Kobin, although I also shot with other brands. Sometimes, I would have to be photographed while in action, which is why it was convenient that I was a soccer player. This was one of my favorite parts of the internship because, although it meant long days out on the field, I got to basically play soccer and spend time with friends while working.
