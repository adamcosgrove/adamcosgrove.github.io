Adam Cosgrove's Internship Final Project

This is my project portfolio for my final project for COMM 393.

This semester, I served as a photographer and social media coordinator from North Carolina currently working with SOCCER.COM. I enjoy taking pictures, especially in a sports setting. In addition to my main responsibility running the Instagram account @soccerdotcom_gk (48k followers), my work has also been published on the SOCCER.COM Instagram (3.7m followers) and Twitter (191k followers) accounts.

I think the main thing that I wanted to get out of this project was new skills. Going in, I figured that would mean improving at my photography and social media skills. However, I was pushed more than that. I learned different aspects of marketing strategy, such as when to put things on sale and how many units to buy of a product. I learned about planning posts on Twitter and Instagram based on analytics gathered from the sites to help determine what will perform well. I, possibly most important to me, also learned new skills in content creation, especially with audio and HTML coding, which feature prominently in my final project.

That, along with some of my other work from this semester, can be found in the menu above. I hope you enjoy!
